Tableau:
Sepsis

Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to analyze biomarker differences between Sepsis and Non-Sepsis patients using clinical dataset from Excel/CSV sources. Built advanced visuals including distribution comparisons, heatmaps, dendrograms, lollipop charts, and radial views to uncover patterns across key biomarkers (Heart Rate, PaCO₂, Temperature, Lactate, WBC). Enabled dynamic filtering by demographics and severity levels to support deeper exploration.
Impact: Identified significantly elevated biomarkers in Sepsis patients, surfaced anomalies for clinical attention, and delivered data-driven insights to support early risk detection and stratification.
